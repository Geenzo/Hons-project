Edinburgh Castle, An 11th-century castle and barracks housing the Crown Jewels and National War Museum of Scotland.

Stirling Castle

Stirling Castle, 12th-century castle and former Renaissance royals palace, with great hall and regimental museum.

National Museum of Scotland, Bright modern museum housing Scottish industrial history relics, natural history displays and cafe.
